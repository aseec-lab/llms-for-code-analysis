The ChainHead class in the androidx.constraintlayout.solver.widgets package is responsible for managing the properties of a chain of ConstraintWidgets.

Properties and functionality of the ChainHead class include:
- Tracking the first and last widgets in the chain.
- Tracking the first and last visible widgets in the chain.
- Keeping count of the number of widgets in the chain and the number of widgets that match the constraints.
- Tracking the total size and weight of the chain.
- Checking if the chain is optimizable.
- Handling RTL (right-to-left) orientation.
- Checking if the chain has defined or undefined weights.
- Handling match constraints and ratio for widgets in the chain.
