The HelperWidget class is a subclass of ConstraintWidget and implements the Helper interface. It is used in ConstraintLayout for grouping and managing a collection of ConstraintWidgets. It has an array of ConstraintWidgets called mWidgets and an integer called mWidgetsCount to keep track of the number of widgets in the collection. The class provides methods for adding widgets, finding dependent widgets, copying widgets, removing all widget IDs, and updating constraints.
